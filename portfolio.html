E-commerce Platform

Project Overview

We developed a full-featured e-commerce platform for a retail client looking to expand their online presence. The solution included product catalog management, secure payment processing, customer account management, and real-time inventory tracking.

Technology Stack

React.js, Node.js, MongoDB, AWS, Stripe API, Elasticsearch

Key Features

  • Responsive design for seamless mobile shopping
  • Advanced search and filtering capabilities
  • Secure payment processing with multiple options
  • Customer account management and order tracking
  • Real-time inventory synchronization
  • Comprehensive admin dashboard for analytics

Results

The platform led to a 150% increase in online sales within the first six months and reduced cart abandonment rates by 35%. The client also reported a significant improvement in customer satisfaction scores.
